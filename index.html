Challenges of being a developer in 2020

  • People were going more remote / flexible
  • The crisis of 2020 has moved things along by decades
  • People / companies are competing with the whole world and not just locally
  • No tech events for networking and meeting new people
  • Currently no face to face

Things were going to change anyway

Going remote, flexible working, extending teams offshore

I have seen this with my clients - hiring developers with DevRel experience as companies know they are competing globally now to hire the best talent

I have had so many job offers from GitHub to Google

What is DevRel? (Developer Relations)

  • Sharing your knowledge
  • Educating the community
  • Getting involved & giving back to the community
  • Helping / mentoring others
  • Encouraging and motivating others

Who has done this? You have all probably done this, so you are all in DevRel. Why not do it a bit more

Techies who are new to the industry say they only have 1 month experience, but you can help someone else getting started

Actions

  • Who is this for? You!
  • Open source (not just about code)
  • YouTube videos / live streams / podtcasts / blog posts
  • Speak at events
  • Join Slack / Discord channels

Why? Hiring a builder/garden, would you believe them say “I can do everything”, or would you prefer to see photos/videos of before/after and documenting the journey

It is not easy, but it is not hard

  • Little every day
  • Sharing an article you read and add value by highlighting the key takeaways
  • Improve yourself by getting feedback
  • Marathon not a sprint

Brushing your teeth. Does it take to long? If you miss a day no big deal. Over time is the benefit

I have seen GitHub accounts with 100 commits in 1 weekend, not consistent, no value to anyone

I hired an offshore person without interview or test - because they had a youtube video teaching something - from this I could not only learn how they explain things and their English, but also their technical thinking (attitude is more important than skill) - no negotiation

Conclusion

  • Not content creation, share your journey and add value
  • You are probably doing it anyway
  • Do a bit more and consistently to get the job you want and the money you deserve
